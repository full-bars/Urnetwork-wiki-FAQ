# Points & airdrop

### What are "Points"?
Points are a parallel reward system to USDC. While USDC is paid out weekly, Points accumulate to determine your share of the future **$UR token airdrop** on Solana.

### Earning Points
There are three primary ways to accumulate points:

| Point Type | Description | Multipliers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Traffic Points** | Earned from moving billable data | Seeker (2x) |
| **Reliability Points** | Earned from uptime per IP address | Country Multiplier, Stability Multiplier (>99.9%) |
| **Referral Points** | Earned when people you refer move traffic | Referral percentages |

### $UR Token Airdrop
* **Conversion:** Accumulated points will be redeemable for the $UR token.
* **Timeline:** The team is actively working on a concrete launch date for the token generation event (TGE).
* **Network Ownership:** The $UR token represents ownership and governance in the URnetwork protocol.

### Reliability Points Calculation
* **Per IP:** Calculated per unique IP address. Each IP can earn a maximum of 1 full reliability point per payout period (representing 100% uptime).
* **Stability Bonus:** Staff confirmed that a multiplier is being added for providers who achieve **>99.9% reliability** for a block. This incentivizes stable, long-term infrastructure.
* **IP Redundancy:** Running 2–3 nodes on the same IP improves your chance of achieving 100% uptime but does not earn "extra" points for that single IP.

### Referral Points
* **Network Effect:** When you refer a user who starts providing bandwidth and moving traffic, you earn a percentage of their points.
* **Limit:** Some accounts may have a maximum number of referrals; check the #support channel if you hit a limit.

> **Staff Reminder:** Points are your ticket to the token airdrop. Focus on stability and low-latency connections to maximize your accumulation.
