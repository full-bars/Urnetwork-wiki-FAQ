# USDC earnings

### Payout Schedule
Payouts are processed every **Sunday at 00:00 UTC**.
* **Automatic:** Funds are sent directly to the Solana wallet linked to your account.
* **Duration:** Processing typically takes about an hour, though delays (sometimes up to a few days) can occur due to API or exchange issues.
* **Currency:** Earnings are paid in **USDC on Solana**.

### Unpaid vs. Paid Data
* **Current Unpaid Data:** Billable traffic you have provided since the last payout cycle. This is your pending balance.
* **Current Paid Data:** History of your previous successful payouts.
* **Settlement:** Every Sunday, your unpaid balance is settled. If it meets the threshold, it is paid out; otherwise, it rolls over.

### Payout Threshold & Fees
There is no fixed minimum payout (e.g., "$1.00"). Instead, payouts are triggered when your moved traffic is sufficient to cover the network's transaction processing fees.
* **Dynamic:** The exact threshold varies week to week based on the payout pool and Solana network conditions.
* **Roll-over:** If you don't earn enough to cover the fee in one week, your balance simply rolls over to the next week. **No data is lost.**

### Multipliers & Earnings
* **Country Multipliers:** Confirmed by staff to apply to both reliability points and USDC earnings.
* **Seeker Multiplier:** Applies to **Points** (for the $UR airdrop), not directly to USDC.
* **Reliability:** Maintaining >99.9% uptime is the best way to maximize your share of the weekly pool.

> **Tip:** If your payout shows as "pending" in the dashboard after Sunday, check your Solana wallet directly (e.g., Phantom, Solflare). The dashboard UI may have a slight display lag.
