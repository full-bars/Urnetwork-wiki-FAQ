# Seeker NFT

### What is the Seeker?
The Seeker is a hardware device associated with a Solana NFT. Owners of the hardware and holders of the NFT receive significant bonuses within the URnetwork ecosystem.

### Activating the 2x Multiplier
To receive the **2x multiplier on payout points**, you must:
1. Hold the **Seeker Genesis NFT** or the **Saga Genesis Token** in your Solana wallet.
2. Link that wallet to your URnetwork account via the mobile app or [web dashboard](https://app.ur.network/account).
3. Verify the link—the multiplier will appear in your points breakdown.

> **Info:** Simply connecting a wallet is not enough; the system performs a real-time check for the presence of the specific NFT/Token.

### Troubleshooting the Multiplier
* **Showing 0x:** The 2x multiplier applies to **payout points**, not directly to USDC. If your weekly traffic earnings are 0, then 2x 0 = 0. Once you start winning contracts and moving traffic, the multiplier will be reflected in your point accumulation.
* **Hardware Signing:** If your token is in a hardware wallet (like Ledger), you may encounter signing issues in the app. Ensure you are using a compatible SDK/wallet provider (Phantom is highly recommended by staff).
* **"Always open with" Error:** If you accidentally set a default app for wallet links on Android, go to **Settings → Apps → Default Apps** and clear the defaults for that app to restore the wallet selection popup.

### Seeker Mobile App
The Seeker phone is highly optimized for providing, but standard iOS and Android apps face background limitations imposed by the OS (Apple/Google). For maximum reliability, wired PC setups or specialized hardware like the URnode are often more consistent.

> **Note:** The Seeker 2x multiplier applies to **Traffic Points** (the points that go toward the $UR airdrop).
