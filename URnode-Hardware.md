# URnode Hardware

### What is the URnode?
The URnode is a dedicated hardware solution (VPN router) designed to simplify connecting devices to the URnetwork without requiring individual app installations.

### Key Features
* **VPN Router:** Acts as a gateway for your entire home network. Whatever devices you connect to the URnode will benefit from the URnetwork VPN tunnel.
* **Pro Plan Benefits:** Staff have indicated that devices connected through a URnode will receive benefits similar to the "Pro Plan" access.
* **Plug-and-Play:** Designed to be easier than managing a PC or Docker-based provider node.

### Pre-order Status
* **Availability:** As of early 2026, the URnode has been available for pre-order. 
* **Shipping:** Shipping timelines have shifted; many users in Discord are awaiting updates on their late 2025/early 2026 orders.
* **Price:** Pre-order pricing has varied (e.g., $95 vs $135), likely due to regional shipping or early-bird tiers.

### Technical Details
* **Connectivity:** Connects via Ethernet directly to your primary router for the lowest possible latency.
* **Alimentation:** Usually powered via USB or a standard power adapter.

> **Note:** You do **not** need an URnode to provide bandwidth on the network. You can achieve the same results using the standard provider binary on a PC or VPS. The URnode is a convenience and optimization product.
