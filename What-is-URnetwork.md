# What is URnetwork?

### What is URnetwork?
URnetwork is a decentralized, peer-to-peer (P2P) VPN protocol. Unlike traditional VPNs that route traffic through centralized corporate servers, URnetwork routes data through a global network of individual provider nodes. This creates a privacy-preserving system that eliminates the need to trust a single provider with your data.

### Roles in the Network
* **Provider:** You share your internet bandwidth. Other users' traffic is routed through your IP address. Providers earn **USDC** (stablecoin) and **Points** (for the future $UR token airdrop).
* **Client:** You use the network to browse privately, routing your traffic through provider nodes. Clients pay a subscription fee or use a "freemium" model which contributes to the provider payout pool.

You can be both a provider and a client simultaneously. In the mobile app, the **"Provide while disconnected"** setting is essential if you want to continue earning even when you aren't using the VPN yourself.

### Economics & Payouts
The network's economy is funded by client subscriptions and prepaid data plans. These funds are distributed to providers based on the **number of contracts handled**, rather than just raw data volume.

* **USDC:** Paid out weekly for moving billable traffic.
* **Points:** Accumulated for reliability, referrals, and traffic, which will later convert to ownership in the **$UR token**.

> **Note:** The network's earning potential is tied to demand. As more clients join and pay for subscriptions, the payout pool for providers increases. Encouraging network growth benefits everyone.

### Circular Traffic
URnetwork has anti-abuse protections to identify "circular traffic" (e.g., connecting to your own provider node via a client on the same account). While this won't get you banned, this traffic is **not credited** for rewards. Rewards are intended for helping *others* connect.
